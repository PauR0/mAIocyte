Miscelanea:
--------------

This case contains the geometry of a slab with dimensions:

- x: 0.03  cm
- y: 2     cm
- z: 0.025 cm

The geometry is described by means of the **nodes.dat** and **elements.dat** files.

File nodes have the format:

        nNdes  1  3
        nNdes NODETYPE
        nId    nType    x y z
